Showtimes brings every cinema in your city into one app — from the big
chains to small independent and arthouse screens. Instead of opening
several websites, you can see what's on today and at what time in just
a few seconds.

Every film shows its IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes and Metacritic scores, so
you can tell a hit from a disappointment at a glance — without hopping
between sites.

What you'll find in Showtimes:
• Every cinema in your city in one place
• Showtimes for today and the days ahead
• IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes and Metacritic ratings on every title
• Posters, titles and the key details for each film
• A fast city search and a clean, card-based layout
• Works for the big cinema chains and local independents alike

Personalisation (once you sign in):
• Hide films you've already seen or aren't interested in
• Turn off cinemas you never visit — keep only your own listings
• Sign in with Google, Facebook or Apple and your settings sync across your devices

You get every listing and rating without signing in — an account only
adds personalisation. Showtimes is free.

See what's on at the cinema in your city today, compare the ratings and
plan your evening — all in Showtimes.
